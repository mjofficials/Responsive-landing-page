About us

Aram Kalaiyagam was founded in the year 2019. It was founded with a vision to create excellence in extracurricular activities. Today we stand as a one-stop destination to conduct 5 courses under 1 roof. Our speciality of courses being Abacus, Keyboard, Guitar, Western Dance, Classical Dance, Karate, Yoga, Archery, Silambam etc. All our programs are conducted by leading experts & stalwarts.
